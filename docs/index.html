hatemail.cc
 Giving hate on science a platform.

Every day hateful, insulting and threatening emails are sent to scientists around the world. The hatemail project aims to collect these messages and raise public awareness of the problem.

Got some fresh hatemail in your inbox?

Just forward to report@hatemail.cc to contribute!


Need more information? See details


To be considered, emails should be forwarded from an academic institution email account. At the moment, only English and German messages can be considered. All personal data will be removed or pseudonymized. Learn more
